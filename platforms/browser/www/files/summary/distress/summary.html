Your Distress








Your scores for psychological distress fall on the higher side, which means that in the last one month, you seem to have experienced significantly high levels of overall distress. This may include feelings of sadness and anxiety, general mental discomfort etc.
High scores on distress might be due to an ongoing stress in your life and often signal a need to seek some kind of help from your friends/family and/or even professional help, as may be required.
When we are distressed, it becomes important to use all our internal and external/social resources to help ourselves. Seeking help that we need does not mean that we are weak. In fact it means we are taking our side, standing up for our self and being our best friend. Take time to take care of your emotional needs.
