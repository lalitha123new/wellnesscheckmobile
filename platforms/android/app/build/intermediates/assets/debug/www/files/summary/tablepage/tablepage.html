Our sincere thanks to you for participating in the pilot phase of the Wellness Check Survey. Your participation has helped the ongoing research on the use of well being tools.

This feedback may be treated as a rough index rather than a rigid/precise categorization- as further research is ongoing.

Different measures of well being assess somewhat different dimensions of well being and they also have different time frames ( e.g. psychological well being taps more general and longer term aspects of psychological functioning while distress scale used taps current levels of distress in the last one month period).Hence it is not expected that the different dimensions correlate very highly. In fact that is the very reason; different measures are used- in order to get a broader picture.

It would be good to hear from you about your experience of participating in this survey.


Please remember well-being levels are changeable -with intentional acts in your journey of growth.
